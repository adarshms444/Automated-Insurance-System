# Policy: Jeevan Raksha Term Assurance
**Category:** Life Insurance
**Policy ID:** LIFE-TERM-009
**Type:** Non-Linked, Non-Participating, Pure Risk Premium Plan

## Product Overview
This is a pure term insurance plan that provides financial protection to the nominee in case of the unfortunate demise of the life assured during the policy term. It is a low-cost, high-cover plan ideal for income protection.

## Death Benefit
In the event of the unfortunate death of the Life Assured during the policy term, the **Sum Assured on Death** will be paid to the Nominee.
* The policy terminates immediately after the death benefit is paid.
* **Accidental Death Rider:** An additional sum equal to the Base Sum Assured is paid if death occurs due to an accident.

## Maturity Benefit
Since this is a pure term plan, **no maturity benefit** or survival benefit is payable if the Life Assured survives till the end of the policy term. There is no surrender value.

## Premium Structure (Sample Rates)
* **Age 25:** ₹4,500/year for ₹50 Lakh Cover.
* **Age 35:** ₹6,800/year for ₹50 Lakh Cover.
* **Age 45:** ₹10,500/year for ₹50 Lakh Cover.
* *Premium payment modes: Annual, Half-Yearly, or Monthly.*

## Eligibility for Recommendation
* **Minimum Entry Age:** 18 Years.
* **Maximum Entry Age:** 55 Years.
* **Maximum Maturity Age:** 70 Years.
* **Income Proof:** Salary Slip or ITR required for cover above ₹25 Lakhs. For lower cover (Saral Jeevan), standard ID proof suffices.

## Suicide Clause
If the Life Assured commits suicide within 12 months from the date of commencement of risk, the nominee will be entitled to 80% of the premiums paid, provided the policy is in force.
