# Policy: Krishi Fasal Bima Yojana (KFBY)
**Category:** Agriculture Insurance
**Policy ID:** AGRI-001
**Regulatory Authority:** AIC (Agriculture Insurance Company of India)

## Product Overview
This policy provides comprehensive insurance cover against failure of the crop thus helping in stabilizing the income of the farmers. It covers food crops (Cereals, Millets, and Pulses), Oilseeds, and Annual Commercial/Horticultural crops.

## Coverage Scope (Risks Covered)
1.  **Prevented Sowing/Planting Risk:** Insured area is prevented from sowing/planting due to deficit rainfall or adverse seasonal conditions. Benefit: 25% of Sum Insured.
2.  **Standing Crop (Sowing to Harvesting):** Comprehensive risk insurance to cover yield losses due to non-preventable risks, such as Drought, Dry spells, Flood, Inundation, Pests and Diseases, Landslides, Natural Fire and Lightning, Storm, Hailstorm, and Cyclone.
3.  **Post-Harvest Losses:** Coverage available up to a maximum period of 14 days from harvesting for those crops which are kept in "cut & spread" condition to dry in the field. Risk covered: Cyclone and Cyclonic rains and Unseasonal rains.
4.  **Localized Calamities:** Loss/damage resulting from occurrence of identified localized risks of Hailstorm, Landslide, Inundation, Cloud burst and Natural fire due to lightning affecting isolated farms in the notified area.

## General Exclusions
* Losses arising out of war and nuclear risks.
* Malicious damage and other preventable risks.
* Losses due to grazing by domestic and/or wild animals.

## Premium Rates (Actuarial vs Farmer Share)
The scheme has a uniform premium rate structure for farmers:
* **Kharif Crops (Rice, Maize, Cotton):** 2.0% of Sum Insured or Actuarial rate, whichever is less.
* **Rabi Crops (Wheat, Barley, Mustard):** 1.5% of Sum Insured or Actuarial rate, whichever is less.
* **Commercial & Horticultural Crops:** 5.0% of Sum Insured or Actuarial rate, whichever is less.
*(The balance premium is paid by the Government as subsidy).*

## Eligibility for Recommendation
* **Land Holding:** Must possess insurable interest in the land (Owner or Tenant).
* **Documentation:** Aadhaar, Bank Passbook, Land Possession Certificate (LPC).
