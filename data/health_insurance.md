# Policy: Gramin Suraksha Family Floater
**Category:** Health Insurance
**Policy ID:** HLTH-RURAL-05
**Network:** 6,500+ Cashless Hospitals

## Product Overview
A comprehensive health protection plan designed specifically for rural families, covering up to 6 family members (Self, Spouse, Dependent Parents, Dependent Children) under a single Sum Insured.

## Key Benefits & Coverage
1.  **In-Patient Hospitalization:** Expenses covered for hospitalization exceeding 24 hours. Includes Room Rent (up to 1% of SI per day), ICU charges (up to 2% of SI per day), Nursing, and Surgeon fees.
2.  **Pre & Post Hospitalization:** Medical expenses incurred 30 days prior to admission and 60 days after discharge are covered.
3.  **Day Care Procedures:** Covers 140+ identified procedures that require less than 24 hours of hospitalization (e.g., Cataract, Dialysis, Radiotherapy, Chemotherapy).
4.  **Ayush Treatment:** In-patient treatment taken under Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy is covered up to 25% of Sum Insured.
5.  **Road Ambulance:** Reimbursement up to ₹2,000 per hospitalization.

## Waiting Periods & Exclusions
* **Initial Waiting Period:** 30 days for all illnesses (except accidents).
* **Specific Diseases:** 24 months waiting period for Cataract, Hernia, Hysterectomy, Joint Replacement, and Kidney Stones.
* **Pre-Existing Diseases (PED):** Covered after 48 months of continuous coverage.
* **Permanent Exclusions:** Self-inflicted injuries, drug/alcohol abuse, cosmetic surgery, and war-related injuries.

## Plan Options & Premium
* **Silver Plan (₹2 Lakh SI):** ₹2,500/year for family of 4.
* **Gold Plan (₹5 Lakh SI):** ₹4,800/year for family of 4.
* **Platinum Plan (₹10 Lakh SI):** ₹8,500/year for family of 4.

## Eligibility for Recommendation
* **Proposer Age:** 18 years to 65 years.
* **Dependent Children:** 91 days to 25 years.
* **Medical Checkup:** Not required up to age 50 (if no history of illness).
